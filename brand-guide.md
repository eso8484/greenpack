# Green Pack Delight - Brand Guide

---

## 1. Company Overview

**Company Name:** Green Pack Delight
**Tagline:** "Discover Local Shops & Services Near You"
**Industry:** Local Service & Shop Discovery Platform (E-Commerce / Marketplace)
**Location:** Lagos, Nigeria

### What We Do
Green Pack Delight is a digital platform that connects customers with local shops and service providers. Unlike traditional e-commerce sites, we don't just facilitate buying and selling — we help customers discover trusted local businesses, view their offerings through short video showcases, and reach out directly for both products and services.

### Our Mission
To empower local businesses by giving them a digital storefront and helping customers find reliable services in their neighborhood — from barbers to laundry, phone repair to fashion tailoring.

---

## 2. Brand Identity

### Brand Personality
- **Trustworthy** — We verify businesses so customers feel safe
- **Community-driven** — Supporting local shops and service providers
- **Fresh & Modern** — Clean, minimal design that feels approachable
- **Energetic** — Green represents growth, freshness, and new beginnings

### Brand Voice
- Friendly and conversational (not corporate)
- Encouraging and supportive of local businesses
- Clear and straightforward — no jargon
- Warm, like recommending a shop to a friend

---

## 3. Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Primary Green:** #22C55E (Fresh, vibrant green — our main brand color)
- **Dark Green:** #16A34A (Used for hover states and emphasis)
- **Light Green:** #86EFAC (Used for backgrounds, highlights)

### Secondary Colors
- **Emerald Accent:** #059669 (For premium elements)
- **White:** #FFFFFF (Clean backgrounds)
- **Light Gray:** #F8FAFC (Section backgrounds)
- **Dark Gray:** #1F2937 (Text color)

### Dark Mode Colors
- **Dark Background:** #111827 (Main dark background)
- **Dark Card:** #1F2937 (Card backgrounds in dark mode)
- **Dark Border:** #374151 (Borders in dark mode)
- **Green on Dark:** #4ADE80 (Brighter green for dark backgrounds)

### Color Usage
- Green is ALWAYS the dominant brand color
- Use green for buttons, links, highlights, and CTAs
- White/light gray for backgrounds
- Dark gray for body text
- Avoid using red except for error states or "out of stock" badges

## 4. Typography

### Primary Font: Inter
- **Headings:** Inter Bold (700 weight)

- **Body Text:** Inter Regular (400 weight)

### Font Sizes
- Hero Heading: 48-60px
- Page Heading: 24-32px

## 5. Logo Requirements

### Logo Concept
We need a logo that represents:
- A green package/box or shopping bag (representing "Pack")
- Combined with a leaf, checkmark, or spark (representing "Delight")
- Modern, minimal, and recognizable at small sizes
- Works on both light and dark backgrounds

### Logo Variations Needed
1. **Full Logo:** Icon + "Green Pack Delight" text
2. **Short Logo:** Icon + "GreenPack" text
3. **Icon Only:** Just the symbol (for favicon, app icon)
4. **Monochrome:** White version for dark backgrounds

### Current Placeholder
- We currently use a green rounded square with the letter "G" in white
- The text reads "Green" (in dark) + "Pack" (in green)

## 6. Imagery Style

### Photography Style
- **Warm, natural lighting** — no harsh studio lights
- **Authentic Nigerian settings** — real shops, real people, real services
- **Diverse representation** — men, women, various ages
- **Action shots** — barbers cutting hair, laundry being done, food being prepared
- **Close-ups of quality work** — detailed shots showing craftsmanship

### Categories That Need Images
1. **Laundry & Dry Cleaning** — Fresh folded clothes, washing machines, delivery
2. **Barbershop & Salon** — Haircuts in progress, clean shop interiors, grooming tools
3. **Phone & Tech Repair** — Phone screens being fixed, tools, before/after
4. **Fashion & Clothing** — Adire fabrics, tailoring, fashion shows, African prints
5. **Food & Restaurant** — Jollof rice, egusi soup, suya, catering setups
6. **Home Services** — Electricians, plumbers at work, tools, installations
7. **Electronics & Gadgets** — Phones, laptops, accessories on display
8. **Health & Beauty** — Spa treatments, skincare products, makeup application

### Hero/Banner Images
- Wide panoramic shots of bustling Lagos markets or modern shop interiors
- Green overlay or gradient effect for brand consistency
- People smiling, engaging with local businesses

### Image Treatment
- Slightly warm color grading
- Green tint or overlay on hero sections
- Rounded corners (12-16px border radius) on all images
- Subtle shadows for depth

---

## 7. Icons & Graphics

### Icon Style
- **Line icons** (outlined, not filled) for UI elements
- 2px stroke weight
- Rounded line caps and joins
- Green color for active/highlighted icons
- Gray for inactive icons

### Graphic Elements
- Rounded rectangles and cards with subtle shadows
- Green gradient backgrounds (from #22C55E to #16A34A)
- Circular avatars for user/shop profiles
- Badge/pill shapes for categories and labels

---

## 8. UI Component Styles

### Buttons
- **Primary:** Green (#22C55E) background, white text, rounded-lg (8px)
- **Secondary:** Light gray background, dark text
- **Outline:** Green border, green text, transparent background
- All buttons have slight scale animation on hover

### Cards
- White background with subtle border (#E5E7EB)
- 12px border radius
- Subtle shadow that increases on hover
- Lift animation on hover (moves up 4px)

### Badges
- Small pill-shaped labels
- Green badge for "Verified" shops
- Yellow for "Popular"
- Gray for categories

---

## 9. Platform Features (For Context)

### What Users See
- **Homepage:** Hero banner, category grid, featured shops, "How It Works" section
- **Browse Page:** Shop listings with category sidebar, search, and filters
- **Shop Profile:** Video showcase, services list, products grid, reviews, contact info
- **Cart:** Items grouped by shop, service inquiries + product orders
- **Checkout:** Contact form to reach out to service providers

### Key Platform Concepts
- Shops can showcase their business with SHORT VIDEOS
- Services AND products — not just buying/selling
- Customers CONNECT with providers (phone, WhatsApp, inquiry form)
- Verified badge system for trusted businesses
- Star ratings and customer reviews
